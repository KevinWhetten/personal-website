My Music

American Fork Symphony

The American Fork Symphony is a member of the Timpanogos Arts Foundation. I joined in 2016 and have been playing Cello with the symphony since then. We do a variety of concerts throughout the year. Every spring, we have a Salute to Youth concert, which features talented young people in the community as soloists. Then, in July, the symphony performs a patriotic concert as part of American Fork's annual Steel Days celebration. In October, the symphony performs a variety show, often including a mix of classical and contemporary pieces. Then, the symphony has a Christmas concert every December where the community is invited to feel the spirit of the season though music. The American Fork Symphony has been a beloved part of the community since 1994.
